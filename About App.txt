1.This is MEETUTU app which shows all the registered users(teachers,learners) who are in one kilometer radius
2.It has less activities which could be easily understood by the user.
3.Registration should be done by the user along with the location which should be shown to other users on map.
4.Location will be taken just by clicking on GET LOCATION button in the signup form by enabling location services.
5.Registration can also be directly done by using user's google account and by giving other info.
6.User will click on a marker which will show the details of the existing user.
7.On clicking the snippet,whatsapp conversatio will be started.
8.On clicking the logout button logs out from the app.
